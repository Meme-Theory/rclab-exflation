INTERPRETATION NOTE
-------------------

D_F as computed here is the geometric Yukawa structure of the Baptista KK
construction -- it encodes the CAPACITY for mass mixing as a function of tau.
In the physical vacuum (gauge fields A^a_mu = 0), the off-diagonal mass mixing
vanishes (Paper 18 eq 7.5) and D_total = D_K (Pfaffian = +1, Session 17c).

The D_F matrix elements measure which modes couple, with what strength, and
with what chiral asymmetry -- analogous to Yukawa coupling matrices in standard
NCG (Approach A) but derived from geometry alone (zero free parameters).

The Pfaffian scan in 30Ab tests whether the geometry PERMITS a topological
transition when gauge fields are active, not whether the vacuum itself
undergoes one. A sign change would indicate that a gauge field configuration
exists where D_total has a zero eigenvalue crossing.

Pfaffian Factorization Structure

  Pfaffian factorizes into 6 independent sector blocks:
  - (0,0): 32-dim block.  Pf = +1 at all tau.
  - (1,0): 96-dim block.  Pf = +1 at all tau.
  - (0,1): 96-dim block.  Pf = +1 at all tau.
  - (2,0): 192-dim block. Pf = +1 at all tau.
  - (0,2): 192-dim block. Pf = +1 at all tau.
  - (1,1): 256-dim block. Pf = +1 at all tau.
  Total = product of 6 sector Pfaffians = +1.

  Each sector pairs with its OWN charge conjugate (D_minus = G5_ext *
  conj(D_plus) * G5_ext). Conjugate sectors (p,q) and (q,p) have
  identical eigenvalue spectra (contragredience) and are computed as
  independent blocks with independently determined Pfaffian signs.

  Conjugate pair consistency (phonon-sim, 12 significant digits):
  - Pf(0,1) = Pf(1,0) at ALL tau values: PASS
  - Pf(0,2) = Pf(2,0) at ALL tau values: PASS
  Despite self-conjugate Xi construction, conjugate pairs produce equal
  Pfaffians. Mechanism (baptista): contragredient sectors have identical
  spectra (rho_{(q,p)} = -rho_{(p,q)}^T). Pf^2 = det(Xi)*det(D), so
  equal spectra => equal |Pf|. Equal signs follow from continuity +
  shared initial condition (Pf = +1 at tau=0 for all sectors).

  Effective factorization:
  Pf(total) = Pf(0,0) * [Pf(0,1)]^2 * [Pf(0,2)]^2 * Pf(1,1)
  Squared terms cannot flip total sign: only (0,0) and (1,1) are Z_2-relevant
  for the TOTAL Pfaffian. Both are +1 at all tau.

  Note: Pfaffian values overflow to NaN at tau > ~1.5 (large matrix norms),
  but the SIGN is determined at all computable tau values and locked in before
  overflow. The sign is the physical observable; the magnitude is not.

6. Interior Mixing Theorem (numerical, N_max=2; algebraic mechanism):
   D_F = sum_a [D_K, L_{e_a}] couples predominantly to interior spectral
   modes, not gap-edge modes. Algebraic mechanism (Paper 17 eq 1.6):
   <psi_m, [D_K, L_X] psi_{m'}> proportional to (m + m'), which vanishes
   when m = m' = 0. Gap-edge modes (smallest |m|) have systematically
   smaller D_F matrix elements than interior modes (large |m|).
   Quantitative: at tau=0.50, ||D_F||/||D_K|| = 0.334 but D_total gap
   reduced by only 4.8% in (0,0) sector. Suppression factor ~9x relative
   to naive operator-norm bound.
   Source: 30Ab, baptista analysis of 30Aa data, Paper 17 eq 1.6.
   Implication: Gap closure via D_F is algebraically suppressed on the
   Jensen curve. The naive ev/gap ratio (0.849 at tau=0.50) dramatically
   overpredicts gap-edge perturbation.
   Surviving solution space: Off-Jensen metrics where gap-edge mode
   identity changes. The T2 direction (29Bb) compresses the C^2 subspace
   where the Jensen gap-edge mode lives, breaking both the mode identity
   and the Killing/non-Killing decomposition that the suppression relies on.

7. Truncation robustness:
   Higher N_max adds interior modes (Weyl's law), further diluting
   gap-edge D_F coupling. The N_max=2 result is conservative: gap closure
   becomes harder at larger truncation on the Jensen curve. No need to
   repeat at N_max=3 on this curve.

8. Xi sector pairing is self-conjugate, not cross-conjugate:
   The real structure operator Xi pairs each Peter-Weyl sector with its
   OWN charge conjugate via D_minus = G5_ext * conj(D_plus) * G5_ext.
   It does NOT cross-link (p,q) with (q,p). This was initially
