PART 4: BCS GAP EQUATION — DIAGONAL CHANNEL (tau-response)
========================================================================

In the eigenstate basis, dD_K/dtau is diagonal:
  V_nm = (d lambda_n/d tau) * delta_nm

The diagonal BCS equation reduces to a mode-by-mode condition:
  |v_n| / (2 * |xi_n|) > 1 for attractive mode n

where xi_n = |lambda_n| - lambda_F, and v_n < 0 (attractive).

DEGENERACY HANDLING: Modes exactly AT the gap edge (xi_n = 0)
are NOT quasiparticles — they ARE the condensate. The BCS criterion
applies to modes ABOVE the gap edge. We use xi_min = first nonzero
eigenvalue spacing as the cutoff.
