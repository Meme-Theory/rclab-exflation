Framework predictions at z~4-8 are degenerate with LCDM. Five questions evaluated:

1. **Stiff epoch (w=1)**: Ends before BBN (~10^{-9} scale factor). Zero impact on D(z) at z~5-8. Cannot help "too massive too early" problem.
2. **GGE relic DM**: sigma/m ~ 10^{-51} cm^2/g. Operationally identical to CDM. 46+ orders from SIDM threshold. No distinctive LRD prediction.
3. **sigma_8 = 0.799**: Reduces LRD host halo abundance by ~3% (nu~1.5 at M_h~10^{10.5}). Below current survey noise. Testable by weak lensing, not LRDs.
4. **32-cell tessellation**: Scale mismatch of 10^{37}. No mechanism maps KK-scale structure to kpc-scale clustering.
5. **D(z) growth factor**: Identical to LCDM post-BBN. Not testable at z~5-8.

**Why:** Framework must be tested via n_s (Window 1, SA-Goldstone mixing), BAO w_0/w_a (DESI), or weak lensing sigma_8 (Rubin) -- not via LRD observations.
**How to apply:** When evaluating framework-LRD connections, the honest answer is "degenerate with LCDM at all observable scales."
