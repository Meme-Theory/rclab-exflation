66 papers (2023-2026) at `researchers/Little-Red-Dots/`. Index at `index.md` with dependency graph, topic map, quick reference table.

**Critical papers for framework evaluation**:
- Paper 13 (Das): Growth timescale as cosmology probe. D(z) cosmology-dependent.
- Paper 30 (Mehta): Modified cosmology vs modified astrophysics. Simons Observatory 10.4sigma by ~2028.
- Paper 38 (Li): Selection bias inflates apparent masses 10-100x. Corrected masses consistent with LCDM.
- Paper 40 (Chon): Heavy seeds form naturally in LCDM without exotic DM.
- Paper 54 (McClymont): Overmassive ratio is demographic artifact (DM-dominated halos).
- Paper 55 (Roberts): SIDM clustering prediction b_eff~4.5 vs CDM b~2-3.

**Mass debate** (unresolved as of 2026): Virial masses 2-3 dex uncertain (Paper 24). E-scattering (Paper 15) vs Paschen ratios ruling it out (Paper 31). First dynamical mass: 50+/-10 million M_sun at z=7.04 (Paper 51).

**SIDM/FDM papers**: 32, 33, 34, 55, 56 -- ALL incompatible with framework (sigma/m mismatch 46+ orders).
