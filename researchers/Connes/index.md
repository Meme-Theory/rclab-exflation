# Connes Paper Index

**Researcher**: Alain Connes + Collaborators (Chamseddine, Marcolli, van Suijlekom, Martinetti, Khalkhali, et al.)
**Papers**: 44 (1980--2026)
**Primary domain**: Noncommutative geometry, spectral triples, spectral action principle, NCG Standard Model, KO-dimension classification, finite-density extensions, twisted spectral triples, random NCG, spectral geometry on Lie groups, spectral action cosmology
**Project relevance**: Connes' NCG program is the mathematical backbone of the phonon-exflation framework. The spectral triple (A,H,D), spectral action, KO-dimension 6 classification, and algebra A_F = C+H+M_3(C) are all Connes constructions verified computationally in Sessions 7-14. Papers 15-16 extend to finite-density thermodynamics. Papers 23-28 address the order-one condition (the project's key axiom violation at 4.000). Papers 30-33,44 develop twisted spectral triples and Lorentzian emergence. Papers 34-35,41 connect to random NCG/bootstrap. Papers 38,36 provide Dirac spectrum methods on Lie groups. Papers 42-43 develop spectral action cosmology.

## Topic Map

### A. NCG Foundations (Mathematical Infrastructure)
Papers: 01, 02, 04, 06
Cyclic cohomology, Dixmier trace, K-theory, Chern character, local index formula, Seeley-DeWitt coefficients, spectral zeta functions. The mathematical tools that make the spectral action computable. Paper 06 (Connes-Moscovici) provides the heat kernel bridge from spectrum to geometry.

### B. Real Spectral Triples and Classification
Papers: 05, 08, 12
The J operator, KO-dimension mod 8, the seven axioms (dimension, regularity, finiteness, reality, first-order, orientability, Poincare duality). The classification theorem: A = M_a(H) + M_{2a}(C), a=2 -> Pati-Salam -> SM. Paper 05 establishes KO-dim 6 = SM. Paper 12 proves uniqueness.

### C. The NCG Standard Model
Papers: 03, 09, 10
The Connes-Lott model (03), its completion with neutrino mixing and seesaw (09), and the definitive CCM 2007 derivation (10). Paper 10 is THE benchmark: full bosonic + fermionic action, GUT relations g_1^2 = g_2^2 = (5/3)g_3^2, Higgs potential from spectral action, cosmological constant.

### D. Spectral Action Principle
Papers: 07, 17, 18
The spectral action Tr f(D^2/Lambda^2), its asymptotic expansion via Seeley-DeWitt, the cutoff function moments f_0/f_2/f_4, and the resulting physics: Einstein-Hilbert + Yang-Mills + Higgs + CC. Paper 07 is the principle; Papers 17-18 demonstrate quantitative precision.

### E. Resilience, Sigma Field, and Higgs Mass
Papers: 13, 20
The 125 GeV Higgs crisis and its resolution via the sigma field (real scalar singlet from Majorana sector). Paper 13 shows m_H in [120,130] GeV after sigma portal correction. Vacuum stability from sigma coupling.

### F. NCG Unification and Open Problems
Papers: 11, 14, 29
Reviews and roadmaps. Paper 11 lists 6 open problems mapping to the project. Paper 14 (spectral standpoint) is Connes' 2019 survey: reconstruction theorem, volume quantization, thermodynamic interpretation, random NCG. Paper 29 is a non-technical review for broader audience.

### G. Order-One Relaxation and Pati-Salam
Papers: 23, 24, 25, 27, 40
Paper 23 removes the order-one condition: quadratic inner fluctuations appear. Paper 24 shows Pati-Salam SU(2)_L x SU(2)_R x SU(4) emerges naturally. Paper 25 introduces weak order-one as middle path. Paper 40 computes gauge unification in spectral Pati-Salam. Paper 27 provides phenomenology (leptoquarks, proton decay).

### H. Entropy and Finite Density
Papers: 15, 16
Paper 15: von Neumann entropy = spectral action with universal cutoff function f_S; connection to Riemann zeta via functional equation. Paper 16: extension to finite chemical potential mu; Bessel function coefficients; KO-dimension preserved. CRITICAL for BCS/finite-density physics on internal space.

### I. One-Loop Corrections and Quantization
Papers: 19, 22
Paper 19 (van Nuland-vS): spectral action renormalizable at one loop; counterterms have same form; gauge coupling unification preserved. Paper 22: synthesis of recent quantum corrections and Lorentzian issues.

### J. Twisted Spectral Triples and Lorentzian Emergence
Papers: 30, 31, 32, 33, 44
Paper 30 (Filaci-Martinetti): critical survey of twists; twist-induced 1-forms bridge Euclidean to Lorentzian. Paper 33: minimal twist SM produces extra scalars + enriched 1-forms. Papers 31-32 (Martinetti): Lorentz symmetry and time EMERGE from almost-commutative twist. Paper 44: twist induces Krein space (indefinite inner product), connecting to twistor geometry.

### K. Random NCG and Dirac Ensembles
Papers: 34, 35, 41
Paper 34 (Khalkhali-Hessam): Dirac operator ensembles, spectral phase transitions, double scaling -> Liouville QG. Paper 35: bootstrap for Dirac ensembles, semidefinite programs, moment problem. Paper 41: large-N fuzzy geometries with fermions, spectral density convergence.

### L. Spectral Geometry on Lie Groups
Papers: 36, 37, 38, 39
Paper 38 (Boldt-Lauret): Dirac spectrum on SU(2), isospectral rigidity -- closest analog to D_K on SU(3). Paper 36 (de Groot): pseudo-Riemannian spectral triples on SU(1,1) via Kostant cubic Dirac. Paper 37 (Hekkelman-McDonald): NC integral on truncated spectra, Szego formula, quantum ergodicity. Paper 39 (Connes-Consani-Moscovici): zeta zeros via prolate wave operators.

### M. Spectral Truncations
Papers: 28
Paper 28 (Connes-vS 2021): rigorous error analysis for Peter-Weyl truncations. Tolerance relations, propagation numbers. Convergence theorems justifying the project's p+q<=6 truncation of D_K.

### N. Spectral Action Cosmology
Papers: 42, 43
Paper 42 (Nelson-Sakellariadou): Higgs as inflaton from spectral action; inflationary parameters from NCG potential. Paper 43 (Marcolli-Pierpaoli): early universe models with sterile neutrino DM, running G_N, conformal gravity at intermediate scales.

### O. Textbook and Pedagogical
Papers: 04, 26
Paper 04 (Connes book 1994): the 661-page treatise. Paper 26 (vS textbook 2ed 2024): graduate-level synthesis through 2024 including Pati-Salam, one-loop, truncations, finite-density.

### Group 1: NCG Foundations (01-06)

**Paper 01** -- Connes (1980). "C*-algebres et geometrie differentielle." CR Acad Sci Paris 290, A599.
6pp founding note. Cyclic cocycles, Chern character, NC integral, Gelfand duality. Launches entire program.
Relevance: HIGH. Dictionary: commutative algebra <-> space. Foundation for spectral paradigm.

**Paper 02** -- Connes (1985). "Non-commutative differential geometry." Publ Math IHES 62, 41-144.

**Paper 03** -- Connes-Lott (1990). "Particle models and NCG." Nucl Phys B Proc Suppl 18B, 29.
First SM from NCG. Product M4 x F, Higgs as internal connection, gauge group from inner automorphisms. Limitations: no nu_R, no spectral action, no gravity.
Relevance: HIGH. Establishes product geometry paradigm.

**Paper 04** -- Connes (1994). Noncommutative Geometry. Academic Press, 661pp.

Relevance: CRITICAL. Operating manual for entire framework.

**Paper 06** -- Connes-Moscovici (1995). "Local index formula in NCG." GAFA 5, 174.
