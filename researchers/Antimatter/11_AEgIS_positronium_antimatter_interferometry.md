# AEgIS: Antimatter Experiment — Gravity, Interferometry, Spectroscopy

**Collaboration**: AEgIS
**Spokesperson**: Ruggero Caravita (INFN Trento); formerly Michael Doser (CERN)
**Location**: CERN Antiproton Decelerator / ELENA
**Active**: 2012–present

## Overview

The AEgIS experiment aims to measure the gravitational acceleration of antihydrogen using a pulsed beam and a moiré deflectometer — a gravity-sensing interferometric technique. Additionally, AEgIS has achieved breakthrough results in positronium physics, including the first laser cooling of positronium.

## Scientific Goals

1. **Primary**: Measure g̅ (gravitational acceleration of H̄) to 1% precision using deflectometry
2. **Secondary**: Positronium spectroscopy and laser cooling
3. **Long-term**: Positronium Bose-Einstein condensate; coherent gamma-ray source

## Experimental Method

### Antihydrogen Beam Production
Unlike ALPHA (which traps atoms), AEgIS produces a BEAM of antihydrogen:

1. Antiprotons from AD/ELENA captured in a Penning trap
2. Positronium (Ps) atoms formed from positrons hitting a nanoporous silica target
3. Ps laser-excited to Rydberg states (principal quantum number n ~ 20-30)
4. Rydberg Ps collides with trapped antiprotons:
   - Ps* + p̄ → H̄* + e⁻ (charge-exchange reaction)
5. H̄* atoms Stark-accelerated to form a horizontal beam

### Moiré Deflectometer
The beam passes through a moiré deflectometer:
- Two identical gratings separated by distance L
- Gravity deflects the beam by Δy = (1/2)g̅(L/v)² during transit
- The deflection pattern on a position-sensitive detector reveals g̅
- Classical (not quantum) interference — works with incoherent beams

Expected deflection: ~20 μm for v ~ 500 m/s, L ~ 40 cm

## Positronium Achievements

### What Is Positronium?
Positronium (Ps) is the bound state of an electron and positron — the lightest "atom" and a pure leptonic system:
- **Para-positronium** (p-Ps): S=0, lifetime 125 ps, decays to 2γ
- **Ortho-positronium** (o-Ps): S=1, lifetime 142 ns, decays to 3γ
- Mass: 2mₑ = 1.022 MeV/c²
- Bohr radius: 2a₀ = 1.06 Å (twice hydrogen's)

### First Laser Cooling of Positronium (2024)
AEgIS achieved the first-ever laser cooling of a positronium gas:
- **Method**: 1S-3P transition at 205 nm, followed by spontaneous emission
- **Result**: Temperature reduced from ~380 K to ~170 K (factor of 2.2)
- **Significance**: Recognized as one of the "Top 10 Physics Breakthroughs of 2024" by Physics World
- **Goal**: Reach < 10 K, enabling precision gravity measurements and eventually a Ps BEC

### Positronium Production
AEgIS's advanced Ps source:
- ~80,000 o-Ps atoms per minute
- Lifetime: 1,140 ns (8× longer than free o-Ps due to reduced annihilation on surfaces)
- Velocity: 70-120 km/s (well-characterized beam)

## Future Prospects

### Positronium BEC
If Ps can be cooled below ~15 mK at densities ~10¹⁸ cm⁻³:
- Bosonic: para-Ps has spin 0, can form a BEC
- All atoms in the same quantum state
- Stimulated annihilation: coherent gamma-ray emission (γ-ray laser)
- Predicted by A. P. Mills (2002); not yet achieved

### Gravity Measurement Timeline
- Phase 1 (current): Demonstrate beam production and deflectometer
- Phase 2: First g̅ measurement at ~10% precision
- Phase 3: Improve to 1% with Rydberg Ps optimization

## Connection to the Framework

### 1. Positronium as Pure QED System
Positronium is described ENTIRELY by QED — no strong or weak interactions. Its energy levels are:

    E_n = -α²mₑc²/(4n²) + QED corrections

In the NCG framework, positronium involves only the U(1) sector of the gauge group. This is a testbed for checking that the spectral action correctly reproduces pure QED without unwanted corrections from the internal geometry.

### 2. Ps BEC and Phonon Analogy
A positronium BEC would be a REAL-WORLD bosonic condensate made of particle-antiparticle pairs. This is precisely the kind of system the phonon-exflation model treats as fundamental:
- The Ps BEC ground state is the "sea" (Dirac/phonon analog)
- Excitations above the BEC are quasiparticles
- The BEC-to-annihilation transition mirrors the phonon condensate's decay channels
- Studying Ps BEC dynamics could directly test phonon-exflation predictions

### 3. Matter-Antimatter Symmetry in Pure Leptonic System
Ps is its own antiparticle (C|Ps⟩ = ±|Ps⟩). This self-conjugate nature maps to the NCG condition:
- In the leptonic sector of H_F, the J operator connects ν, e⁻ with ν̄, e⁺
- Positronium states involve coherent superpositions across this J boundary
- The para-Ps/ortho-Ps split tests the spin-statistics connection that the spectral triple must reproduce
