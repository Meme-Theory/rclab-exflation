# Antimatter: Open Questions and Framework Connections

## The Seven Great Antimatter Questions

### 1. Why Is There More Matter Than Antimatter?
**Status**: UNSOLVED — the baryon asymmetry problem
- The SM provides all three Sakharov conditions but CP violation is ~10 orders of magnitude too small
- Leading candidates: leptogenesis, electroweak baryogenesis (BSM), Affleck-Dine
- **Framework connection**: The phonon-exflation mechanism provides a non-equilibrium process (Sakharov condition 3). Can the Jensen deformation encode CP violation geometrically?

### 2. Does Antimatter Fall the Same Way as Matter?
**Status**: PARTIALLY ANSWERED — ALPHA-g (2023) confirms a_g/g = 0.75 ± 0.29, consistent with g
- Rules out gravitational repulsion at > 99.97% CL
- Precision must improve to < 1% to be meaningful for WEP tests
- **Framework connection**: WEP guaranteed in KK framework if J commutes with the 4D metric sector. ALPHA-g confirms this.

### 3. Is CPT Exact?
**Status**: No violation detected — tested to 16 ppt (q/m), 2 ppt (1S-2S)
- Any CPT violation would require abandoning either Lorentz invariance or locality
- **Framework connection**: The NCG real structure J IS CPT. Its defining relations (J² = +1, JD = DJ, Jγ = -γJ) are the algebraic encoding. Every CPT test is a test of J.

### 4. Can We Store Antimatter Long-Term?
**Status**: Months (antiprotons in Penning traps), hours (antihydrogen in magnetic traps)
- BASE has stored antiprotons for > 400 days
- ALPHA traps H̄ for hours
- **Application**: Portable antimatter for off-site experiments (BASE-STEP)

### 5. Can Antimatter Be Used for Energy?
**Status**: Impractical — production cost vastly exceeds annihilation energy
- Cost: ~$62.5 trillion per gram of antihydrogen (estimated)
- Energy: 1 gram H̄ + 1 gram H → 1.8 × 10¹⁴ J = 43 kilotons TNT equivalent
- Production efficiency: ~10⁻⁹ (for every billion protons accelerated, ~1 antiproton usable)
- **Framework connection**: Not directly relevant, but annihilation energy = 2mc², which in the NCG framework traces to the D_K eigenvalue spectrum

### 6. What Is the Gravitational Field of Antimatter?
**Status**: OPEN — WEP tested (attracted by Earth), but does antimatter GENERATE gravity identically?
- Active vs. passive gravitational mass distinction
- No experiment has measured the gravitational field PRODUCED by antimatter
- GR predicts identical gravitational fields for matter and antimatter
- **Framework connection**: In the KK framework, the gravitational field is generated by the energy-momentum tensor, which is symmetric under J. Active = passive mass is a structural prediction.

### 7. Are There Antimatter Stars, Galaxies, or Domains?
**Status**: No evidence — cosmic ray, gamma-ray, and CMB data all consistent with matter-dominated universe
- Annihilation at domain boundaries would produce ~MeV gamma rays — not observed by Fermi-LAT
- Limits: antimatter fraction < 10⁻⁵ within ~10 Mpc
- **Framework connection**: The baryon asymmetry is a cosmological boundary condition that the framework must eventually address
