Classical cosmology and black hole physics are dominated by **Hawking radiation**—continuous, thermal, semi-classical. The Hawking-Page phase transition (Paper 35) and AMPS firewall argument (Paper 36) both assume this is the fundamental particle-creation mechanism.

The phonon-exflation framework proposes a **different regime** where:
- **Particle creation** is via Schwinger effect (non-perturbative, field-dependent, threshold-suppressed—Paper 33)
- **No black holes** exist (no Hawking radiation, no evaporation, no information paradox)
- **No firewall** needed (equivalence principle preserved, S_ent = 0)
- **Phase transition** is geometric (fold in SU(3) fiber), not thermodynamic (like Hawking-Page)
- **Entropy** is encoded in integrals of motion (Richardson-Gaudin), not lost to radiation

### Cosmological Implications

The Darabi (Paper 34) mechanism shows that compactification and cosmic acceleration are coupled: one drives the other. The framework interprets the observed cosmic acceleration not as a cosmological constant but as the **ongoing effect of a fold transition in extra dimensions**.

If the fold is dynamical (ongoing or recent), then:
- Dark energy is not constant but evolving (w ≠ -1)

- GW background has distinctive features (energy concentrated at fold-transition frequencies)

DESI data (Paper 30 context) hints at w ≠ -1, suggesting dynamical dark energy. The framework's prediction aligns with this observational hint.

### Information Paradox Resolution

Papers 35-36 frame the core tension: equivalence principle vs. unitarity vs. smoothness. The framework's answer:
- **Equivalence principle**: Preserved (smooth fold, no tachyons)

The trick: **no evaporation**. By avoiding Hawking radiation (the source of the paradox), the framework sidesteps the trilemma. This is not a "loophole" but a fundamental difference in what particle-creation mechanism operates in the early universe.
