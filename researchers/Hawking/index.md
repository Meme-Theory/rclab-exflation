## Topic Map

### A. Singularity Theorems & Area Theorem
Papers: 01, 02
The geometric foundation: Hawking-Penrose singularity theorem proves geodesic incompleteness under energy conditions + trapped surfaces. Hawking's area theorem (delta A >= 0) establishes the classical second law of black hole mechanics via Raychaudhuri equation.

### B. Four Laws & Black Hole Thermodynamics
Papers: 03, 04, 05, 11, 35
The exact structural correspondence between black hole mechanics and thermodynamics. BCH four laws, Bekenstein entropy S = eta*A/l_P^2, Hawking radiation T = hbar*kappa/(2pi), and the Hawking-Page phase transition in AdS. Paper 05 is the central technical achievement: Bogoliubov coefficients |beta|^2/|alpha|^2 = exp(-2pi*omega/kappa) yielding exact Planck spectrum.

### C. Information Paradox & Resolution
Papers: 06, 10, 13, 14, 18, 21, 36
The complete arc from Hawking's 1976 non-unitarity claim through AMPS firewalls to the island formula resolution. Paper 06 introduces the superscattering operator $. Paper 13 defines the Page curve benchmark. Papers 14 (Penington) and 21 (replica wormholes) derive the Page curve from semiclassical gravity via quantum extremal surfaces.

### D. Cosmological Particle Creation
Papers: 15, 16, 19, 25, 27, 29
Parker's foundational program: quantized fields in expanding universes produce particles via Bogoliubov mixing without horizons. Part I (scalars), Part II (fermions, all spins). Ford review synthesizes 50 years of applications. Kolb-Long catalogs gravitational particle production across all spins. Fulling-Davies provides the moving mirror analog.

### E. Thermodynamic Gravity & Spectral Entropy
Papers: 17, 20, 22, 32
Jacobson derives Einstein equations from delta Q = T dS at local Rindler horizons. Wald generalizes black hole entropy to Noether charge for any diffeomorphism-invariant theory. Chamseddine-Connes-vS proves spectral action = von Neumann entropy of fermionic second quantization. Verlinde proposes gravity as entropic force.

### F. Cosmological Horizons & Quantum Cosmology
Papers: 07, 08, 09
Gibbons-Hawking temperature T = H/(2pi) for de Sitter horizons via Euclidean path integral. Hawking derives inflationary perturbation spectrum delta rho/rho ~ H^2/phi_dot. Hartle-Hawking no-boundary proposal: wave function from Euclidean path integral over compact geometries.

### G. Unruh Effect & Observer Dependence
Papers: 12
Unruh discovers T = hbar*a/(2pi*c*k_B) for accelerating observers. Establishes thermofield double structure, observer-dependence of particle concept, and the deep connection between horizons and temperature.

### H. Islands in Cosmology & de Sitter
Papers: 23, 24, 28, 30, 31
Extension of the island formula beyond black holes: cosmological FRW models, de Sitter space, compactified extra dimensions. Engelhardt-Wall QES provides the formal foundation. Active debate on whether Page curves exist for cosmological horizons (Teresi yes, Shaghoulian no).

### I. KK, Analog & Phase Transitions
Papers: 26, 33, 34, 35
Steinhauer's BEC analog Hawking radiation experiment. Yamada's KK Schwinger effect (pair production below KK threshold). Darabi's dynamical compactification with coupled 4D acceleration. Hawking-Page phase transition in AdS.
