## Topic Map

### A. Vortex Moduli on Riemann Surfaces (Abelian)
Papers: 01, 04, 10, 11, 12
Abelian vortex equations on line bundles over compact Kahler manifolds. Moduli spaces,
L2 metrics, Bradlow limit, singular metrics, degenerate metric interpretation. Mathematical
foundation for Baptista's geometric thinking but not directly used in the KK program.

### B. Topological Gauged Sigma Models
Papers: 03, 05, 06
Topological field theories from gauged sigma models. Hamiltonian Gromov-Witten invariants,
equivariant cohomology of toric manifolds via mirror symmetry. Mathematical physics
infrastructure.

### C. Non-Abelian Vortices and Hecke Modifications
Papers: 07, 08, 09
Non-abelian vortex equations for U(n) gauge fields. Internal structure of vortex zeros via
local matrix factorization. Connection to Hecke modifications, singular monopoles, and
geometric Langlands. L2 metric and Kahler form computations on moduli spaces.

### D. KK Bosonic Sector and Internal Symmetries
Papers: 13, 15, 29
Einstein-Hilbert action on M4 x SU(3) with left-invariant metrics. Gauge bosons from
isometries, massive bosons from weaker symmetries (action-preserving but not metric-preserving).
Higgs mechanism from metric deformation. Jensen deformation parameter tau. Submersion
formalism for gauge boson masses. Scalar curvature R_g formula (eq 2.40 in paper 13).

### E. KK Fermionic Sector and Chirality
Papers: 14, 17
Dirac equation on M4 x K with submersion metrics. 64-component spinor encodes one
generation. Chiral interactions from non-Killing vector fields on K. Avoids traditional KK
chirality no-go theorems. Kosmann-Lichnerowicz derivative central to fermion mass terms.

### F. KK Test Particles and Geodesics
Papers: 16
Geodesic motion on M4 x K with general metrics. Variable mass and charge from massive
gauge field interactions. Constants of motion in mass-preserving regions. Geometric source
of mass variation rate. Lightlike higher-dimensional geodesics.

### G. CP Violation from KK Geometry
Papers: 18, 34
Three geometric sources of CP violation: mass/representation misalignment, non-minimal
fermion-massive-gauge couplings, non-abelian Pauli terms. Dimensional reduction of free
massless Dirac equation naturally violates CP. Bourguignon-Gauduchon spinor map used.

### H. Spectral Action Principle and NCG
Papers: 21, 19, 27, 57
Foundational spectral action Tr f(D/Lambda). Gauge theory from unbounded Kasparov
product. Hilbert bundle structure, inner fluctuations as connections. sin^2(theta_W) = 3/8
at GUT scale. Van Suijlekom textbook (2ed) covers finite-density, KK-NCG bridge.

### I. Entropy and Finite-Density Spectral Action
Papers: 20, 31, 41
Von Neumann entropy equals spectral action for specific universal function. Heat kernel
coefficients involve Riemann zeta values zeta(3), zeta(5). KMS states at finite temperature
and chemical potential. Dong-Khalkhali extends to bosonic second quantization with mu!=0.

### J. Gauge Coupling Running and Thresholds
Papers: 22, 24
KK threshold corrections in warped geometry (exponential suppression). Weinberg angle
evolution in 5D with KK tower contributions. One-loop beta functions modified by KK states.

### K. Pati-Salam and Exceptional Unification
Papers: 23, 26, 30, 35
Spectral Pati-Salam from NCG. SU(2)_L x SU(2)_R x SU(4)_c from spectral triple algebra.
Automatic coupling unification at ~10^16 GeV. Exceptional groups G2, E8 via nonassociative
bimodule gauge transformations. Leptoquark phenomenology.

### L. KK-NCG Bridge and Normalization
Papers: 25, 32, 33, 36
Three normalization conventions (GUT/Dynkin, Killing, Democratic) and transformation
formulas. Heat kernel on product spaces M4 x K x F. Seeley-DeWitt factorization. KK and
NCG are mathematically equivalent through heat kernel bridge.

### M. Explicit KK Reduction Examples
Papers: 28
Explicit 6D Einstein gravity reduction on S2. Normalized Killing vector method. Rank-deficient
gauge kinetic matrix from coset geometry. Template for SU(3) reduction.

### N. Lichnerowicz Stability of Einstein Metrics
Papers: 37, 38, 39, 48, 56
Lauret I/II: universal formula for Lichnerowicz Laplacian on G-invariant TT tensors via
Casimir operators. Schwahn: 51 new stable Einstein examples. Destabilisation of warped
product Einstein metrics. Derdzinski-Gal: rigidity of bi-invariant metric on SU(2n+1).
DIRECTLY determines whether Jensen deformation endpoint is stable or unstable.

### O. KK Spectrometry and Holographic RG Flow
Papers: 42, 43, 44, 51, 52
Squashed S7 as model for deformed internal geometry. Exceptional field theory computes
full KK spectra beyond consistent truncations. Domain wall solutions connecting squashed to
round metrics. Cesaro: Dirac/Laplacian spectra on squashed S7 as function of squashing.
Duff-Pope review of 40 years of KK supergravity.

### P. Geometric Flow on SU(3)
Papers: 45, 46
Ricci flow on SU(3)/T flag manifold: four invariant lines, each to a distinct Einstein attractor.
Cheeger deformations on fiber bundles interpolating between metrics. Directly relevant to
tau-evolution of left-invariant metrics on SU(3).

### Q. BCS on Curved Spaces
Papers: 47
Superconductivity on hyperbolic geometry. Edge-localized pairing, boundary DOS enhancement.
Bogoliubov-de Gennes equations on curved manifolds. Key reference for BCS on SU(3).

### R. Observational Tests (DESI)
Papers: 49, 50
DESI DR1: 2.5-3.9sigma tension with LambdaCDM. w_0 > -1, w_a < 0. DR2: ~3sigma
confirmation of dynamical dark energy. Direct test of phonon-exflation prediction w = -1.

### S. Supplementary KK and NCG References
Papers: 53, 54, 55
De Saxce: charge as coadjoint orbit classification. DKKT: Weinberg angle from discretized
KK (sin^2 theta_W = 0.231). Fatibene: Lie derivative of spinor fields via reductive G-structures
and Kosmann lift.

## Notation Conventions

### Baptista (Papers 13-18)
- **kappa**: AdG-invariant inner product on su(3), kappa = alpha * Tr(uv)
- **alpha**: positive scaling factor in kappa
- **sigma**: deformation parameter in C^2, |sigma|^2 < 1/4
- **g_sigma**: deformed left-invariant metric on SU(3)
- **A_L, A_R**: one-forms on M4 with values in su(3) (left/right gauge fields)
- **S**: second fundamental form of fibres (NOT shape tensor S in some references)
- **F**: interlacing tensor (called A in O'Neill/Besse)
- **N**: mean curvature vector of fibres
- **Delta_12**: 12D spinor space, identified with M_{8x8}(C)
- **e^L_j, e^R_j**: left/right-invariant vector fields on K
- **chi**: involution Ad_xi on su(3), xi = diag(1,-1,-1)
- **u(2) + C^2**: eigenspace decomposition of su(3) under chi
- Metric signature: (-,+,...,+) Lorentzian

### NCG (Papers 19-21, 57)
- **D**: Dirac operator on spectral triple
- **Lambda**: energy cutoff scale
- **f**: smooth cutoff function
- **a_n**: Seeley-DeWitt coefficients
- **A_F**: finite algebra of SM spectral triple
- **D_F**: Dirac operator on finite space (encodes Yukawa/Higgs)
- KO-dimension: 6 for SM

### Lauret/Schwahn (Papers 37-39)
- **G/K**: compact homogeneous space (K = isotropy)
- **g = k + p**: Lie algebra decomposition
- **C_G(rho)**: Casimir eigenvalue of representation rho in G
- **Delta_L**: Lichnerowicz Laplacian
- **TT**: transverse-traceless tensors (traceless + divergence-free)
- Stability: min spec(Delta_L|_TT) >= 0
