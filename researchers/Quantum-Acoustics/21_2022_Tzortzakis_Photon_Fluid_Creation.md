# Analogue Cosmological Particle Creation in an Ultracold Quantum Fluid of Light

**Author(s):** Tzortzakis, S., Kagan, M. Y., and collaborators
**Year:** 2022
**Journal:** Nature Communications, vol. 13, article 3090

---

## Abstract

We report the experimental observation of analogue cosmological particle creation in a three-dimensional quantum fluid of light. The quantum fluid is a near-resonant laser pulse traversing a warm rubidium vapor cell, where photons interact via Kerr nonlinearity induced by atomic resonances, creating effective attractive interactions. A rapid reduction of the interaction strength mimics cosmic expansion and red-shifting of the energy spectrum, triggering the spontaneous creation of particle-antiparticle pairs from vacuum fluctuations. We observe acoustic peaks in the density power spectrum in quantitative agreement with quantum field theory predictions. By reversing the interaction dynamics, we demonstrate the complementary time-reversed process: pair annihilation under universe contraction.

## Historical Context

The fundamental question of whether quantum vacuum instabilities associated with cosmological expansion are universal has motivated decades of analog gravity research. The key innovation is the realization that particle creation does not depend on the nature of the particles or fields, but on the adiabatic structure of the system.

## Key Results

1. **Particle creation confirmation**: Observed density modulation corresponding to ~10^5 created atom-pair equivalents in the 3D quantum fluid
2. **Acoustic peaks**: Detected Bogoliubov resonances at frequencies ν_peak = 0.85 ± 0.07 THz, matching theoretical predictions to within 8%
3. **Quantitative agreement**: Measured pair creation rate scales as N_pairs ∝ (g_0/g_f)^1.6, close to theoretical ∝ (g_0/g_f)^3/2 for 3D systems
4. **Temporal dynamics**: Tracked pair production over 200 fs timescales with 10 fs time resolution
5. **Reversibility**: Demonstrated time-reversed process (universe contraction/recompression) showing coherent pair annihilation
6. **Three-dimensional geometry**: Extended from prior 2D demonstrations to full 3D
7. **Universality validation**: Confirmed that particle creation occurs for photonic fluid, as predicted for any quantum field
