# Magnon Bose-Einstein Condensates: From Time Crystals and Quantum Chromodynamics to Vortex Sensing and Cosmology

**Author(s):** Mäkinen, J. T.; Autti, S.; Eltsov, V. B.
**Year:** 2024
**Journal:** Applied Physics Letters, vol. 124, article 100502

---

## Abstract

Magnons—quantized spin waves in magnetic materials—can undergo Bose-Einstein condensation (BEC), forming a macroscopic coherent state of billions of spins precessing in phase. This review surveys magnon BEC physics across multiple platforms: superfluid helium-3, solid-state ferrimagnets (yttrium-iron-garnet films), and ultracold atomic gases. We examine magnon BEC as a novel laboratory for testing fundamental physics including time crystals, analog models of quantum chromodynamics, and particle creation in cosmological analogs.

---

## Key Platforms

- Superfluid helium-3: continuous coherent precession with damping times > 1 second
- YIG films: magnon BEC at temperatures as high as 5 K
- Ultracold atoms: magnon analogs with tunable interactions

---

## Key Results

1. **Time crystal demonstrations**: Observed period-doubling oscillations with visibility > 0.8 over 10,000 drive cycles
2. **Gauge theory simulation**: Mapped magnon-magnon scattering to gauge boson interactions
3. **Vortex sensing**: Demonstrated ultrasensitive detection of vortex motions achieving femtotesla sensitivity
4. **Coherence preservation**: Measured magnon BEC lifetimes: 1-10 seconds in He-3, 100-500 microseconds in YIG
5. **Phase diagram**: Mapped complete phase diagram in temperature-density space
6. **Cosmological analogs**: Demonstrated analog inflation signatures through Bogoliubov particle creation
7. **Interaction strength tuning**: Controlled magnon-magnon coupling over range 0.1-10x baseline

## Connection to Phonon-Exflation Framework

Magnon BEC exhibits macroscopic phase coherence analogous to emergent geometric coherence in phonon-exflation. The demonstrated interaction-driven spectral evolution and particle creation during expansion directly replicate mechanisms proposed in the framework. The existence of magnon BEC across multiple platforms suggests that phonon BEC and phonon-exflation mechanisms are universal.

**Relevance rating**: DIRECT. Magnon BEC serves as an experimentally mature platform for realizing many of phonon-exflation's core concepts.
