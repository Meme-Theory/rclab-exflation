Volovik's extended framework proposes two Planck-like constants:

1. **Temporal Planck constant**:
   $$\hbar_{	ext{ac}} = c_s a$$

   where c_s ~ 200 m/s in 4He and a ~ 10^-10 m. Thus hbar_ac ~ 10^-8 s.

2. **Spatial Planck constant**:
   $$\ell_{	ext{ac}} = a$$

$$ds^2 = 0 \quad 	ext{(dimensionless)}$$

$$ds^2 = -\hbar_{	ext{ac}}^2 dt^2 + \ell_{	ext{ac}}^2 (dx^2 + dy^2 + dz^2)$$

The spectrum of acoustic excitations (phonons) in a superfluid is:

$$E_n = n \hbar_{	ext{ac}} c_s / a = n \hbar c_s$$

Quantized in units of action hbar_ac * omega_1 where omega_1 = c_s/a is the atomic-scale frequency.
