## Narrative Notes

### Items 8-9: K-1e Double Retraction (S23a → S33b → S34)

The most procedurally significant retraction chain in the project. Session 23a closed the Kosmann-BCS mechanism with V(B2,B2) = 0 using only the C^2 subspace (4 of 8 su(3) generators). This seemed decisive. Ten sessions later, S33b discovered the full 8-generator Kosmann kernel gives V(B2,B2) = 0.287, reopening the channel. The K-1e closure was retracted and a new closure candidate (TRAP-33b) with M_max = 2.06 was announced. Then S34 immediately retracted TRAP-33b: it used frame-space structure constants (V = 0.287) rather than spinor matrix elements (V = 0.057). The correct spinor V eventually gave M_max = 1.445 (later revised to 1.674). Sagan applied a 0.6x epistemic penalty to S33b upward revision for the K-1e retraction procedural cost. The session-34 master synthesis explicitly names this the "most procedurally honest session in the project's history." Lesson: distinguish the vector space carefully — frame V and spinor V are different mathematical objects.

### Item 2: The "4-5x Coupling" Phantom (S21b → S22b)

The inter-sector coupling claim was generated by computing the Kosmann norm within a single sector, not matrix elements between sectors. The block-diagonal theorem (S22b) proved D_K is exactly block-diagonal in Peter-Weyl — making ANY inter-sector coupling algebraically zero. This retraction established Wall W2, the most structurally important wall in the closure map.

### Item 16: GGE Permanence (S38 → S39)

The "Ordered Veil" paradigm shift in S38 introduced the claim that the post-transit GGE relic is permanent (thermalization time ~ Hubble time) because the BCS Hamiltonian is Richardson-Gaudin integrable. S39 found that V_phys is 13% non-separable by Frobenius norm, producing Brody beta = 0.633 (63% GOE character). The system thermalizes in ~6 natural time units. The 8 conserved quantities claimed in S38 were the N_pair=1 sector eigenprojectors, which are correct integrals only within that sector — not global constants of motion. The retraction removed the "only known particle-creation mechanism producing a permanent non-thermal relic" claim and the "horizonless thermalization" mechanism from the open channels.

### Item 15: Schwinger-Instanton Duality (S38 → S39)

The 2.4% agreement between S_Schwinger and S_inst was announced in S38 as a structural duality. S39 showed this is a three-way numerical conspiracy: the GL quartic overestimates both Delta_0 and S_inst by factors of 2.1x and 4.2x respectively, and their ratio happens to cancel for SU(3) at this specific transit speed. In the GL limit, S_inst/S_Schwinger = 4.08, not 1. Nazarewicz independently retracted his WKB identity claim. The result was reclassified from "structural identity" to "numerological coincidence."

### Items 17-19: DM Classification Cycle (S42-S44)

S42 claimed lambda_fs = 3e-48 Mpc (CDM from geometry) using the wrong velocity. S43 retracted this: lambda_fs = 89 Mpc (hot dark matter). Sagan discounted the S42 probability contribution. ### Item 25: The B_1D = 20.9 Inversion (S49 → S50)

S49 reported B_1D = 20.9, suggesting the framework is strongly preferred over LCDM in w_0 space. This appeared to be a major positive. S50 W2-D traced it: B_1D compared against the DESI combined-fit derived parameter w_0 = -0.752, not against raw BAO distances. Against raw BAO data, the framework's w_0 = -0.509 gives chi^2/N = 23.2 (FAIL threshold: 4). The inversion from apparent strong positive to decisive negative is the starkest reversal of fortune in the project's final sessions.
