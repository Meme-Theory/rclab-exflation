## CC Workshop Round 1 Positions (Session 43)

### Diagnosis
The 113-order CC gap is the standard CC problem restated: computing vacuum energy from the effective theory (spectral action) without the microscopic theory of gravity. Paper 05 equilibrium theorem correctly nullifies S(0) but leaves GGE perturbation energy unsuppressed.

### Key Proposals
1. **CC-GGE-GIBBS-44** (CRITICAL): Generalized Gibbs-Duhem identity for 8-mode GGE. The GGE maximizes entropy subject to 8 conserved charges. If these are q-theory vacuum variables, self-tuning applies per-charge. Could suppress rho_grav far below naive rho_GGE.

2. **N3-BDG-44** (HIGH): B2 flat band (W=0 exact) creates Fermi point in BdG spectrum. Paper 04 theorem: Fermi point with N_3 != 0 gives automatic vacuum energy cancellation. B2 carries 85.5% of GGE occupation.

3. **Sakharov Induced Gravity** (NEW, uncomputed): G_N should be DERIVED from Paper 07 formula using 992 KK eigenvalues, not assumed. This may dissolve CC problem entirely.

4. **CDM-RETRACTION-44** (HIGH): S43 lambda_fs=89 Mpc may conflate internal c_q with 4D v_group. S42 CDM classification (lambda_fs=3e-48 Mpc) used 4D dispersion. Retraction itself needs re-examination.

5. **LIFSHITZ-ETA-44** (HIGH): Type I Lifshitz anomalous dimension eta~0.036 (free theory) gives n_s=0.964, within 0.1% of observed. Needs interacting computation.
